About Us

Welcome to Bibliomania, the bookstore dedicated to serve you best. Established in 2024 by Faheema Peerbhai and Antonio Redelinghuys, our store has been a haven for book lovers in Bethlehem ever since. We're passionate about reading, and we're committed to providing our customers with such a community.

Whether you're a longtime reader or just discovering the joy of books, we invite you to explore our shelves and join our community of book enthusiasts.
